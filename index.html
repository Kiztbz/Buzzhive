# Buzzhive


If we had to describe an average student in today's world confussed and stressed would be an understatement. The world is all about competition these days, but to survive and score good in such an environment one must understand how to deal with the overwhelming burden of syllabus. We here at Buzzhive insist you to start from the very basics to get on with the problems and syllabus as efficiently as possible. At Buzzhive we post all the solutions to all the problems that we solve. All topics are dealt and solved with a student point of view hoping that anyone reading a analysing the solutions will easily understand the concept.

# Who are we?


We are a group of people that are in the same situation as you. Yes we are students and hence we write solutions as briefly as we can so that none of our fellow students are left behind.
